Experience

Schneider Electric Internship --- Project Sherlock

During my time at Schneider I took on the challenge of building my first web app.

The goal of the internship was simple: Design a "landscape monitor" allowing Schneider's analysts to better keep track of top competitors.

The solution was mine to come up with, so I decided to build a web app.

By the end of the Summer I had built Sherlock with Apache, PHP, and MySQL.

The idea behind Sherlock:

All the competitive intelligence Sherlock would need could be found internally.

All of the information in Sherlock is contributed by Schneider employees

Information on competitors that an employee finds in the news, from colleagues, or online, can easily be posted on Sherlock.

Other users can then vote, comment, and share this information.

Sherlock provides each user with a feed of competitor information, and the ability to sort their feed any way they like.

Users can also opt in for email updates on information under specific categories, competitors, countries, and/or divisions.
